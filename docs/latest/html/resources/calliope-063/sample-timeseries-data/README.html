Data sources¶

These data are not intended to represent a real energy system and are included for illustrative purposes only.

csp_resource.csv¶

All three time series give heat output in kW per m2 solar field, based on free 2005 DNI data from HelioClim (HC3v2), processed with the CSP plant model described by 1.

demand-1.csv and demand-2.csv¶

Both time series are based on modified data from the IIASA GAINS model database 2.

References¶

1

Gauché, P., von Backström, T.W. & Brent, A.C., 2011. CSP Modeling Methodology for Macro Decision Making - Emphasis on the Central Receiver Type. In Granada, Spain.

2

Amann, M., et al., Cost-effective control of air quality and greenhouse gases in Europe: Modeling and policy applications. Environmental Modelling & Software, 2011. 26(12): p. 1489-1501.
